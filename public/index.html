YouTube Channel

  1. Go to the YouTube channel you want to extract.
  2. Copy the full channel URL (example: https://www.youtube.com/@username).
  3. Paste the URL in the input field Below.
  4. Click Fetch Videos.
  5. Wait until extraction completes.
  6. Click Download CSV if you want the file.

Supported formats:

  • https://www.youtube.com/@username
  • https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCxxxx
  • https://www.youtube.com/c/customname
  • https://www.youtube.com/user/legacyname
